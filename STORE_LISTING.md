## App Store (English)

**App name (≤30):**
MiniTerminal: SSH Client

**Subtitle (≤30):**
SSH, SFTP, keys & terminal

**Promotional text (≤170):**
A fast, secure SSH client for your pocket. Manage servers, run a full
terminal, transfer files over SFTP, and forward ports — your
credentials never leave your device.

**Keywords (≤100, comma-separated):**
ssh,sftp,terminal,ssh client,server,console,shell,ssh key,port
forwarding,bastion,devops,sysadmin

**Description (≤4000):**
MiniTerminal is a fast, secure SSH client for iPhone and iPad. Manage
all your servers and connect from anywhere with a full interactive
terminal in your pocket.

KEY FEATURES
• Host management — organize servers with groups, tags, and search
• Authentication — password or SSH key (generate ed25519 in‑app or
  import your own), with optional passphrases
• Interactive terminal — real PTY shell with color themes, adjustable
  font size, and a handy extra‑key bar (Esc, Tab, Ctrl, arrows)
• SFTP — browse remote folders and upload/download files
• Port forwarding — local and remote tunnels
• Jump host — connect through a bastion
• Multiple sessions — keep several servers connected at once
• Snippets — save and run common commands
• App lock — protect the app with Face ID / Touch ID / passcode

PRIVACY BY DESIGN
MiniTerminal has no accounts and no servers of its own. Your passwords
and private keys are stored only on your device, in the iOS Keychain.
Nothing is collected, tracked, or sent to us.

**What's New:**
Initial release.

**描述 (≤4000):**
MiniTerminal 是一款面向 iPhone 和 iPad 的快速、安全的 SSH 客户端。
随时随地管理你的所有服务器,把完整的交互式终端装进口袋。

主要功能
• 主机管理 —— 用分组、标签和搜索整理服务器
• 认证 —— 密码或 SSH 密钥(应用内生成 ed25519 或导入自有密钥),
  支持口令
• 交互式终端 —— 真实 PTY,配色主题、字号调节、常用键工具条
  (Esc、Tab、Ctrl、方向键)
• SFTP —— 浏览远程目录,上传/下载文件
• 端口转发 —— 本地与远程隧道
• 跳板机 —— 经堡垒机连接
• 多会话 —— 同时连接多台服务器
• 命令片段 —— 保存并一键执行常用命令
• 应用锁 —— 用 Face ID / Touch ID / 密码保护应用

隐私优先
MiniTerminal 没有账号,也没有自己的服务器。你的密码和私钥只保存在
设备本地的 Keychain 中。我们不收集、不追踪、不上传任何数据。
