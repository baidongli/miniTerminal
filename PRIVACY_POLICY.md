# Privacy Policy for MiniTerminal

**Effective date:** 2026-05-22

MiniTerminal ("the app") is an SSH client for iOS and Android. This
policy explains what the app does and does not do with your data. In
short: **MiniTerminal has no user accounts, no backend servers, and
collects no personal data. Everything you enter stays on your device.**

## Data the app stores (only on your device)

- **Host details** you create (label, hostname, port, username, group,
  tags, and connection options) are saved in the app's local storage on
  your device.
- **Passwords, SSH private keys, and key passphrases** are stored in the
  operating system's secure keystore — the **iOS Keychain** or the
  **Android Keystore** — on your device only.

This information never leaves your device through the app. There is no
sign-in, no cloud sync, and no MiniTerminal server that receives your
data.

## Data the app does NOT collect

- No accounts, names, emails, or contacts.
- No analytics, telemetry, usage tracking, or advertising identifiers.
- No location data.
- No third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

## Network connections

MiniTerminal connects only to the **SSH/SFTP servers that you
configure**. Any commands, files, and credentials you send go directly
from your device to those servers over the SSH protocol. The developer
operates no intermediary server and cannot see this traffic.

## Device permissions

- **Local Network** (iOS): required to reach SSH servers on your local
  network.
- **Biometrics / Face ID / device passcode**: used only to unlock the
  app when you enable the optional app lock. No biometric data is read
  or stored by the app; authentication is handled entirely by the
  operating system.
- **File access**: used only when you choose to import an SSH key file
  or upload/download files over SFTP, on files you select.

## Data sharing

MiniTerminal does not sell, share, or transmit your data to the
developer or any third party.

## Data retention and deletion

All data is stored locally. To delete it, remove hosts/keys within the
app or uninstall the app, which removes all locally stored data,
including items in the secure keystore.

## Children's privacy

MiniTerminal is a developer/IT utility and is not directed at children.
It collects no personal information from anyone, including children.

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at the
